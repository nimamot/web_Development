Chose between straight-through cable and crossover:

  • if the device acts like a PC NIC: transmitting on pins 1 and 2 → Straight through cable → the nodes use opposite pairs for transmitting data.
  • if the device acts like a switch: transmitting on pins 3 and 6 → Crossover cable. → the nodes use the same pairs for transmitting data.
